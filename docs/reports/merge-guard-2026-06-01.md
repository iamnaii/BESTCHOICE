### Description
Phase 5 of canned-response admin. Adds `QuickReplyPostbackRouterService` that
handles `TEMPLATE:<id>` payloads — when a LINE/FB customer taps a Quick Reply
button whose payload matches this format, the bot sends the referenced canned
response template back. Wired into both `LineOaChatbotController` (LINE) and
`FacebookWebhookController` (FB). Frontend adds a template-picker dropdown in
`QuickReplyEditor` so staff can assign a template without typing the raw
payload ID.
