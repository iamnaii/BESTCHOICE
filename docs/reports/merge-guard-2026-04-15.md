**Evidence:**

**Impact:** IEEE 754 floating-point arithmetic on financial amounts. For sums across many payments (5,000+ records), rounding errors can accumulate. Violates the v3/v4 hardening rule: all `Decimal` fields from Prisma must use `Prisma.Decimal` arithmetic.
